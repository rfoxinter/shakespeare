Act 3

Scene 8

[Enter Caesar with his army, and Taurus, marching.]



Caesar Taurus!


Taurus My lord?


Caesar

Strike not by land, keep whole. Provoke not battle

Till we have done at sea. Do not exceed

The prescript of this scroll. [Hands him a scroll.]

Our fortune lies

Upon this jump.

[They exit.]
